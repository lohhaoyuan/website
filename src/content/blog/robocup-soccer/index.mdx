## Intro
As a member of [Hwa Chong Robotics](https://instagram.com/hcrobo), I compete in RoboCup Junior Soccer under [*Sentinels Robotics*](https://github.com/senrobo), where we design autonomous 2v2 soccer robots. Our team's performance at the recent [Robocup Singapore Open 2025](https://2025.robocupsg.org) was atrocious to say the least, and it was largely my fault for being a pathological procrastinator. 

This blog post and its various subposts aim to serve as a form of accountability. I'll post regular devlogs to force myself to do work. And if we do perform well, I guess this can serve as a resource for other teams in years to come, assuming that I somehow don't write absolute bullshit.
